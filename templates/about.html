03 : About me

Foundations in Neuroscience

I started my Neuroscience journey by working as a researcher in UCSF studying patients with multiple sclerosis. After four years collaborating with amazing scientists and publishing novel findings, I transitioned to biotech, to further learn about the drug discovery process, an field of research that feels a bit more impactful to the patient population.

Data Science and Machine Learning Journey

My first eye opening experience with machine learning came from working at UCSF. We collabed with a group that specialized in AI therapeutics for projects like retinal imaging, translatable animal behavior, and eye movement. After being part of these studies, I devoted my time to expanding my technical skills in data science and machine learning to complement what I learned performing statistical analyses.

Future Ambitions

My current goal is to dive deeper into the realm of engineering in hopes of bringing my DS and ML passion to this universe. Visit my LinkedIn, my Kaggle, or Github to keep updated on my journey!
